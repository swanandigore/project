Famous Drawings

Drawing, as a visual art form, uses various instruments to create marks on a two-dimensional surface, most commonly paper. These instruments can include pencils, pens, paints, brushes, and more. Drawing can be used to depict objects, ideas, and emotions, and it plays a fundamental role in visual communication and artistic expression. Drawing is a visual art that uses an instrument to mark paper or another two-dimensional surface. The instruments used to make a drawing are pencils, crayons, pens with inks, brushes with paints, or combinations of these, and in more modern times, computer styluses with graphics tablets or gamepads in VR drawing software.
